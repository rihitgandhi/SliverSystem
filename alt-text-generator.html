💡 Best Practices for Alt Text

  • Keep it concise but descriptive (aim for 125 characters or less)
  • Don't start with "Image of" or "Picture of" (screen readers announce it's an image)
  • Include relevant context that's not visible in surrounding text
  • For decorative images, use empty alt text: alt=""
  • For complex images (charts, diagrams), consider long descriptions
