Our Mission

Computer Accessibility

The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect

Our mission

We aim to make technology accessible to everyone, ensuring the web is easier to use for people with disabilities. We teach you to remeber that when you post something online, you have a responsibility to make it accessible to as many people as possible, including the elderly and disabled, so they can fully participate in the vast universe of the internet.

How it works

We offer 10 courses for small business owners and anyone looking to make their website accessible to all. We also provide a series of courses for individuals with disabilities to help them navigate the internet, ensuring everyone can access the web.

How we help the online community
For people with disabilities and the elderly, we help them learn to navigate their way through the internet, helping more people join the web ensuring that everyone gets to try using the web as a learning tool. For people looking to make a website and/or small businesses, we will provide courses to help them make their website accessible for all!
