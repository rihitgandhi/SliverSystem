"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect." - Tim Berners-Lee

Our Mission

We aim to make technology accessible to everyone, ensuring the web is easier to use for people with disabilities. When you post something online, remember that you have a responsibility to make it accessible to as many people as possible, including the elderly and disabled, so they can fully participate in the vast universe of the internet.

Our Mission

We assist people with disabilities and the elderly in navigating the internet, enabling more individuals to use the web as a learning tool. Additionally, we offer courses to help individuals and small businesses create websites that are accessible to everyone.
