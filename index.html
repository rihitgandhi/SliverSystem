Computer Accesibility

"The power of the Web is in its universality. Access by everyone regardless of disability is an essential aspect"
-Tim Berners Lee



Our mission
We aim to make technology accessible to everyone, ensuring the web is easier to use for people with disabilities. When you post something online, remember that you have a responsibility to make it accessible to as many people as possible, including the elderly and disabled, so they can fully participate in the vast universe of the internet.
